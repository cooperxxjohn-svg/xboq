The Problem

You Don't Know What's Missing Until It's Too Late

Indian tender drawings are often incomplete. The gaps become your problem after you win.

📋

Incomplete Drawing Sets

Missing schedules, absent specifications, unclear dimensions. You bid on assumptions — then reality hits during execution.

Scope creep risk
⚠️

Hidden Scope Gaps

No time to manually review every page of every drawing. Critical gaps slip through. You discover them mid-project when it's expensive to fix.

Variation order fights
❓

RFIs Come Too Late

By the time you realize you need clarification, you've already committed. Asking questions after award weakens your position.

Post-bid surprises
🚫

No Go/No-Go Framework

Every tender looks the same from the outside. You need a systematic way to assess bid risk before you invest time and money.

Blind bidding
